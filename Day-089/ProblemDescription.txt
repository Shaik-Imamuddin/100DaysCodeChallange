Introduction
The first century spans from the year 1 up to and including the year 100,
the second century - from the year 101 up to and including the year 200, etc.

Task
Given a year, print the century it is in.

Examples
1705 --> 18
1900 --> 19
1601 --> 17
2000 --> 20
2742 --> 28

Sample Tests:

Testcase-1:
Input:1999
Output:20

Testcase-2:
Input:2021
Output:21

Testcase-3:
Input:2000
Output:20

Testcase-4:
Input:1888
Output:19

Time Complexity:

In CenturyFromYear.py

All methods have a time complexity of O(1), 
making them equally efficient in terms of computational complexity.
