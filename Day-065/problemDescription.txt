DESCRIPTION:

Combine strings program

write a program named (combine_names) that accepts two inputs (first and last name). The program should print the full name.

Example:
'James', 'Stevens'

returns:
'James Stevens'

Sample Tests:

Testcase-1:
Input:hello 
how are you
Output:hello how are you

Testcase-2:
Input:am
i
Output:am i

Testcase-3:
Input:happi
bday
Output:happi bday

Time Complexity:

In CombineStrings.py

All approaches have a time complexity of O(n),
where n is the total length of the combined strings.
