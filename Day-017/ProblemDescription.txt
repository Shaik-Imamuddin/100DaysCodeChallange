Description:
The cockroach is one of the fastest insects.
write a program which takes its speed in km per hour
and returns it in cm per second, rounded down to the integer (= floored).

Sample Tests:

Input:1.08
Output:30

Input:50
Output:1388

Input:0
Output:0

Input:10
Output:277


Time complexity:

In KMperHourToCMperSecond.py The time complexity for all the above methods is 
O(1), which means they all have constant time complexity regardless of the input size.
