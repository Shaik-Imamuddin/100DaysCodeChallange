DESCRIPTION:

Print the number (count) of vowels in the given string.

We will consider a, e, i, o, u, A, E, I, O, U as vowels for this Kata (but not y).

The input string will only consist of lower case and Upper case letters.


Sample Testcases:

Testcase-1:
Input:Apple
Output:2

Testcase-2:
Input:dolbyaudio
Output:5

Testcase-3:
Input:
Output:0

Testcase-4:
Input:baseball
Output:3

Testcase-5:
Input:copycat
Output:2

Time Complexity:

all the methods have a time complexity of O(n), 
making them efficient for counting vowels in a sentence.
