Timmy & Sarah think they are in love, but around where they live, they will only know once they pick a flower each. 
If one of the flowers has an even number of petals and the other has an odd number of petals it means they are in love.

Write a program that will take the number of petals of each flower and print true if they are in love and false if they aren't.

Sample Tests:

Testcase-1:
Input:45
57
Output:False

Testcase-2:
Input:12
13
Output:True

Testcase-3:
Input:677
454
Output:True

Testcase-4:
Input:12
34
Output:False


Time Complexity:

In OppositesAttract.py

All three approaches having a time complexity of O(1).
